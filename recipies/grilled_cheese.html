Grilled Cheese

A grilled cheese is a hot sandwich made with as little as bread, butter and cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your desired bread
  • Butter
  • American cheese
  • Gouda

Steps

  1. Butter one side, of each slice of bread
  2. In a cold pan place 1 slice of bread butter side down
  3. Turn pan on low and start stacking cheese
  4. Throw on your top piece and cover while cooking
  5. Pour a tiny bit of water and close again to create steam
  6. Once browned, flip and repeat process
  7. Take off heat once browned and melted
  8. Cut in half and enjoy!
