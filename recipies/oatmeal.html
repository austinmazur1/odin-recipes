Oatmeal

Oatmeal dates back all the was to 7000 B.C

The oatmeal we eat today, wasnt see till the

ancient Greeks.

Oatmeal is an excelent source of fiber, reduces blood sugar

provides antioxidants, and much more.

Ingredients

  • 40g rolled oats
  • 30g protein powder, flavor of choice
  • 10g powdered peanut butter
  • Dash of cinnamon
  • Nuts
  • Salt
  • 1 banana

Steps

  1. Weigh out 40g of oatmeal, add water and mircowave 2 minutes
  2. Once cooked, add your dry ingredients
  3. Give it a good mix and add water if necessary
  4. Mix in pinch of salt and the nuts
  5. Top with banana
  6. Enjoy!
